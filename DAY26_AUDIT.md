## Recommended "critical for Day 26" list

Given the one-day scope, my recommendation is to treat **#1 (billing unimplemented)** as an escalation to flag for Simha Teja's scope-check rather than something to code today — it's a multi-day build, not a bug fix. Realistic candidates to actually fix today:

- **#2** (unsigned impersonation token) — cheap to sign properly now, before anything depends on the insecure version.
- **#3** (Retell webhook fail-open) — small, contained change.
- **#4** (voice campaign no-rollback) — small, contained change, mirrors an existing pattern.
- **#5** (Apollo cap not enforced) — contained if you're comfortable picking where to enforce it (I'd suggest at the `searchApollo`/`enrichLead`/`enrichLeads` call sites, checking against `campaign.max_leads` capped at `APOLLO_ENRICHMENT_CAP`).

#6–#11 are lower stakes and can be deferred past today without much risk.
